VFX & Compositing Process

Detailed text breakdown of the Visual Effects pipeline.

The Concept

The goal of this project was to seamlessly blend digital elements into real-world footage. The primary focus was on realistic lighting and shadow matching to ensure the VFX felt grounded in reality.

1. Camera Tracking

The process started with analyzing the raw footage. I used 3D Camera Tracking to calculate the movement of the camera. This created a virtual camera that moved exactly like the real one, allowing me to place objects in 3D space.

2. Compositing Layers

Once the tracking was done, I worked on the layers. This involved separating the background from the foreground using Roto-brushing. This is a crucial step to place effects behind characters or objects.

3. Final Polish

In the final stage, I added Color Grading to match the tones. I also added slight Grain and Motion Blur to the digital elements so they matched the imperfections of the original camera lens.
