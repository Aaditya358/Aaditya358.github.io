VFX & Compositing Breakdown

Project Type: Visual Effects | Software: After Effects & Blender

The Process

The workflow began by filming the raw footage. The main challenge was to integrate CGI elements seamlessly into the live-action shot.

Step 1: Tracking
I imported the footage into Adobe After Effects to generate a 3D camera track. Once the tracking data was solved with a low error rate, I exported it directly to Blender.

Step 2: Compositing
After rendering the 3D assets, I brought them back into After Effects for final compositing, color correction, and adding motion blur to match the original camera movement.
