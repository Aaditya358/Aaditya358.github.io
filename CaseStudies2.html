CGI & Object Cleanup

Project Type: 3D Modeling & Clean-up | Software: Blender

The Process

This project focused on removing unwanted objects from a scene and replacing them with high-fidelity CGI models.

Step 1: Modeling & Texturing
In Blender, I modeled the 3D objects with high-poly details. I used PBR textures to ensure the lighting reacted realistically within the environment.

Step 2: Integration
The challenge was alignment. I aligned the models precisely within the scene geometry and optimized the render settings (Cycles) to balance quality and render time.
